About

I am a climate scientist at the French National Research Institute for Sustainable Development (IRD). My expertise includes climate dynamics, large-scale atmospheric teleconnections and climate impacts on human health. In my research, I have focused on extreme climate events (heatwaves, droughts), tropical and subtropical precipitation changes, large-scale atmospheric teleconnections and impacts of extreme heat on human health and mortality. I am interested in understanding how the ongoing climate change is affecting the "livability" and "habitability" of our planet due to extreme heat, from occupational health and sports to human survivability limits.
