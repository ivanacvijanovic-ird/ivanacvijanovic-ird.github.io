Outreach

In collaboration with colleagues from ISGlobal, we have developed an online Heat Index Calculator. You can use it to estimate how dangerous conditions are in your home (you only need temperature and humidity). We also provide resources on how to recognize heat related illness. The Heat Index Calculator accompanied the publication of the paper "Importance of humidity for characterization and communication of dangerous heatwave conditions" that was widely reported upon ( ).

In March 2026, in collaboration with Just Atonement Inc. and the UN Special Rapporteur on Climate Change and Human Rights, I co-organized the side event: "Tipping Points and Human Rights" during the 61st session of the UN Human Rights Council. For more information, see my presentation "Do we need a legal framework for loss of habitability due to extreme heat?" Recording of the event is avaialable here.
